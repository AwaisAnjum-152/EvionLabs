What we build

3D printing, product design and electromechanical development.

Use one service on its own — a single print, a single PCB — or hand us the whole problem and we'll carry it from idea to finished product.

01 — 3D Printing

FDM, resin and SLS printing, in every material we stock.

We print functional parts, enclosures, jigs, fixtures and display models — matched to the material that actually suits the part's job, not just what's cheapest.

See material pricing
02 — Product Design & Development

From a rough sketch to a manufacturable 3D model.

Industrial design, parametric CAD modelling, and design-for-manufacture reviews, so the part that gets printed is also the part that can eventually be produced at volume.

  • Concept sketching and industrial design direction
  • 3D CAD modelling and technical drawings
  • Design-for-manufacture (DFM) and tolerance review
03 — Prototyping & R&D

Idea-to-product research, before you commit to a build.

We run structured research and iteration cycles — feasibility checks, functional prototypes, and test builds — to catch problems while they're still cheap to fix.

  • Concept validation and feasibility research
  • Functional and appearance prototypes
  • Iterative testing across multiple print or build cycles
04 — PCB, Embedded Automation & Simulation

The electronics side of the same product.

PCB schematic and layout design, embedded firmware for automation and control, and simulation work to validate a design before it's built in hardware.

  • PCB design and layout
  • Embedded automation and control systems
  • Electromechanical integration and simulation
