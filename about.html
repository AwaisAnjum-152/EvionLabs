About EvionLab

A design-and-build studio for people who need a real, physical answer.

We started EvionLab because too many good product ideas stall at the sketch stage — stuck between "we can design it" and "we can actually make it." We do both, in the same workshop.

Why EvionLab exists

Design and fabrication, kept in the same room.

Most projects lose time in the handoff — between the person who designs a part and the person who has to actually print, wire, or assemble it. At EvionLab, the same team scopes the design, models it, prints or builds it, and tests it. Fewer handoffs, fewer surprises.

We work with individuals, startups and small manufacturers across Lahore who need a working prototype or a short production run — not a slide deck.

How we work

Mechanical and electronic, treated as one problem.

A product is rarely just a shape or just a circuit — it's both, plus the software that ties them together. Our process moves between CAD, PCB layout, firmware and simulation as needed, instead of treating them as separate projects with separate timelines.

That's why our services list includes 3D printing next to embedded automation and PCB design: in most of our projects, they happen in the same week.

What guides the work

A short list of things we hold ourselves to.

Principle 01

Right material, not default material

We choose PLA, ABS, PETG, resin, nylon or carbon-fibre based on what the part has to survive — not habit.

Principle 02

Show the math, not just the model

Pricing is itemised by weight, material and process, so you can see exactly what you're paying for.

Principle 03

Prototype before you commit

We push for a cheap, fast iteration before a final one — it's almost always cheaper than getting it wrong once.

Want to see what we can build for you?

Tell us about the part, the product, or the problem — we'll tell you how we'd approach it.
